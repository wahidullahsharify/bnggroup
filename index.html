Who We Are

About BNG Foundation

BNG Foundation is a nonprofit, non-political, and non-governmental organization committed to improving lives across Afghanistan. For over 15 years, we have supported vulnerable individuals and communities through a wide range of educational, humanitarian, and development programs.

Founder’s Message

"From the earliest days of BNG Foundation, our mission has been driven by a deep sense of responsibility toward those in need. Witnessing hardship and poverty up close inspired us to act—not as an institution, but as fellow humans extending a hand. This foundation began with small acts of support and has grown into a movement touching thousands of lives annually. I am proud of what we have accomplished together and remain hopeful for an even greater impact in the years to come."

- General Director & Founder, BNG Foundation

Our Mission

To empower communities through education, humanitarian aid, and development programs that promote dignity, self-reliance, and long-term well-being.

Our Vision

A society where every individual, regardless of their background or hardship, has the opportunity to thrive with dignity and purpose.

Our Programs

What We Do

We provide comprehensive services in key areas to build a better future for communities in need.

Education

We pay school and exam fees, help students prepare for university, and support long-term learning and capacity building.

Humanitarian Aid

We provide grocery packages to over 3,000 people annually, distribute Iftar meals during Ramadan, and offer support cards for families in need.

Women Empowerment

We run vocational training programs (e.g., sewing) for widows and assist them in launching small businesses for independence.

Community Development

We support families in rebuilding their lives after crises and implement local programs to improve long-term community resilience.

Health Support

We provide assistance to patients with severe conditions, such as kidney disease, and help families access urgent medical treatment.

And much more...

Our work is constantly expanding to meet the needs of the communities we serve.
